Introduction

Treatment & Dosage Analyzer is a R program running on Shiny that helps to determine the sgnificance and effectiveness of treatment supplements and dosage levels.

Treatment & Dosage Analyzer was the result of a need by budding data scientists to find answers for cause-and-effect questions on various datasets. Indeed, it is specific for this specific category of questions and is a powerful visual tool for evaluation.


Treatment & Dosage Analyzer is implemented in R and Shiny, both Open Source, publicly available software packages supported by large communities of users.

Sample Display

Treatment & Dosage Analyzer easily shows the effectiveness of treatments by dosage, supplement or both

User will be able to slice and dice the data in various ways representative of how the Treatment & Dosage Analyzer works. We have made the user interface easy to use, and reactive to changes in user input.

Source Data


This dataset has 60 observations of tooth growth and is based on a study using supplements of either Orange Juice (30 observations) or Vitamin C (30 observations) at three different dosage levels: 0.5mg, 1.0mg, and 2.0mg (10 observations each).
